Check Your Score for Free

The good news is, you can easily check your credit score for free right from your mobile banking app. It's quick, convenient, and secure.

How to Check:

  1. Open your mobile banking app
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Look for the 'Credit Score' option
  4. View your score provided by TransUnion

Note: Your credit score is available once you log in and is provided by TransUnion, a reliable credit reporting agency.
